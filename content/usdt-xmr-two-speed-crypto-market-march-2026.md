## Stablecoin Policy Trend: More Rules, Not Fewer

The U.S. **GENIUS Act** (S.1582) became **Public Law 119-27 on 18 July 2025**. Whatever one’s politics, the direction is clear: stablecoin rails are moving deeper into formal oversight.

For traders, this has a practical meaning. USDT remains central to routing, but compliance posture now influences availability, transfer friction, and platform behaviour more directly than before.

In other words, stablecoins are becoming more embedded in mainstream infrastructure while privacy-sensitive assets remain selectively constrained. That is the two-speed market.
